Movie Watchlist 2022

I've been writing down each movie I watch in a diary, so I went back over the year and compiled a list for 2022

I watched less than I'd hoped to through the year, but I did see some great ones. In particular Sonatine a very funny and absurdly poetic negative space gangster movie by Takeshi Kitano
and Aftersun a heartbreakingly beautiful film about the memory of a father and daughters holiday together with amazing cinematography and a particularly excellent use of found footage from Charlotte Wells
