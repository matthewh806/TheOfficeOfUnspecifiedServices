Seasonal mixes

These mixes serve as a time capsule for myself, I pick up so much new music every day, week, month that it gets hard to keep track of.

I'll have songs on HEAVY rotation for weeks on end and then I'll abruptly stop listening to them because I've moved onto something else.
So this is really just an attempt at documenting my listening habits and weaving in field recordings & snatches of sound I've picked up here and there
