Imagined Pages

This is a page to explore, imagine, dream of some of the pages I've thought about adding to this wonderful website but have so far either been too lazy or incompetent to create.

I invite you to settle down with a tea or coffee, read the descriptions of the pages below (multiple times if necessary), then close your eyes and try to visualise the experience.

Sound Library

I've become somewhat of a digital horder over the years I think... it started off with wanting to find little snippets of sound on the internet to sprinkle through songs
This quickly gave way to an obsession with accumulating an external hard drives worth of digital detritus.

The actual hard drive itself is like a bomb site, just no organisation whatsoever. But I'd like to play the role of an archivist and expose some of this junk to the wider world.

Like as a jukebox of sound? Or what about some kind of map which groups sounds by similarity?

Eccojam generator

I love the Chuck Person's Eccojams Vol. 1 album and have dabbled in making that heavily chopped & screwed music myself over time.

There is quite a specific process to creating one of these ecco's, almost algorithmic in a sense.

  • Find a song ripe for eccojammin
  • Extract a small loop which expresses some kind of melancholic or wistful longing
  • Pitch shift it down to rinse this feeling for all its worth
  • Drown it in delay and reverb
  • Repeat the loop until everyone loses the will to live

So, quite a simple recipe. The hardest part is finding that little snatch of audio which really stabs you in the heart. Not a good job for a computer... but what if we could ask it to do the rest for us?
